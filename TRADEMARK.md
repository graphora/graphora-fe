# Graphora Trademark Policy

This document outlines the policy for using the Graphora name and trademarks.

## Purpose

The Graphora trademarks, service marks, and logos (collectively "Graphora Marks") are owned by Arivan Labs. This policy exists to:
- Protect the identity and reputation of the Graphora project
- Prevent confusion about the source and quality of Graphora software
- Allow legitimate use of Graphora Marks by the community
- Prevent misleading or harmful uses

## What Are the Graphora Marks?

The Graphora Marks include:
- The name "Graphora"
- The Graphora logo and visual designs
- The names of Graphora products and services
- Graphora taglines and slogans

## Permitted Uses

You **may** use the Graphora Marks without prior approval for:

### 1. Referring to the Software
- Accurate references to the Graphora software in documentation, articles, blog posts, and presentations
- Example: "This application uses Graphora for knowledge graph construction"

### 2. Community Activities
- User groups, meetups, and conferences about Graphora
- Example: "Seattle Graphora Users Meetup"
- Note: Must make clear that these are community-organized, not officially sponsored by Arivan Labs

### 3. Compatible Products and Services
- Describing compatibility or interoperability with Graphora
- Example: "DatabaseX connector for Graphora" or "Graphora-compatible data importer"
- Requirements:
  - Must be factually accurate
  - Must not imply official endorsement
  - Your own branding must be more prominent than Graphora Marks

### 4. Educational and Informational Use
- Teaching, tutorials, courses, and educational materials about Graphora
- Research papers and academic publications
- News articles and commentary

## Prohibited Uses

You **may not** use the Graphora Marks for:

### 1. Commercial Redistribution
- ❌ Offering Graphora as a commercial service under the name "Graphora" without a commercial license
- ❌ Creating a hosted SaaS platform called "Graphora Cloud" or similar
- ❌ Exception: You may offer commercial services if you have a commercial license from Arivan Labs

### 2. Confusingly Similar Names
- ❌ Using "Graphora" in your company name, product name, or domain name in a way that suggests official affiliation
- ❌ Examples of prohibited use:
  - "Graphora Pro"
  - "GraphoraDB"
  - "graphora.io" (official domains)
- ✅ Examples of permitted use:
  - "YourCompany's Graphora Integration"
  - "Consulting services for Graphora"

### 3. Modified Versions
- ❌ Using the Graphora name for modified or forked versions of the software
- ✅ Instead: Use a clearly different name and state "based on Graphora" or "fork of Graphora"
- Example: "GraphForge (based on Graphora)"

### 4. Quality Degradation
- ❌ Using Graphora Marks in ways that harm the project's reputation
- ❌ False or misleading statements about Graphora
- ❌ Implying official endorsement when none exists

### 5. Logo Modifications
- ❌ Modifying, altering, or combining the Graphora logo with other marks without permission

## Special Cases

### Forks and Derivatives

If you fork or create a derivative of Graphora:
- **MUST** use a different name that doesn't include "Graphora"
- **SHOULD** clearly state it's based on or derived from Graphora
- **MAY** state "Based on Graphora by Arivan Labs" in documentation
- **MUST** comply with the AGPL v3 license terms

Example: "GraphWeaver - a knowledge graph tool based on Graphora"

### Commercial Services

If you want to offer Graphora as a commercial service:
- **Contact Arivan Labs** for a commercial license agreement
- Commercial licenses allow use of the Graphora name for your service
- Email: `support@graphora.io`

### Database Vendors and OEMs

If you want to integrate Graphora into your product:
- **Contact Arivan Labs** for an OEM license agreement
- We offer special licensing for database vendors and platform providers
- Email: `sales@graphora.io`

## When in Doubt

If you're unsure whether your use is permitted:
- **Ask first**: Email `support@graphora.io`
- We're generally permissive for community and educational uses
- We want to support the ecosystem while protecting the brand

## Reporting Misuse

If you see misuse of the Graphora Marks, please report it to `support@graphora.io`.

## Additional Permissions

For uses not covered by this policy, or to request explicit permission:
- Email: `support@graphora.io`
- Include: Description of intended use, duration, and distribution
